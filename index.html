What is Cosmetic Dentistry?

Cosmetic dentistry encompasses a variety of dental procedures aimed at improving the aesthetic appeal of a person’s smile. Unlike general dentistry, which focuses primarily on oral health and hygiene, cosmetic dentistry emphasizes the visual aspects of dental care. Common procedures include teeth whitening, veneers, bonding, and orthodontics. At Markham Gateway Dentistry, patients can expect personalized treatment plans that cater to their specific goals and concerns

Popular Cosmetic Dentistry Services at Markham Gateway Dentistry

Markham Gateway Dentistry offers an extensive range of cosmetic services, ensuring that patients can find the right solution for their dental needs. Here are some of the most popular cosmetic procedures available at the clinic:

1. Teeth Whitening

One of the most sought-after cosmetic procedures is teeth whitening. Over time, teeth can become stained or discolored due to various factors, including diet, smoking, and aging.

  1. In-Office Whitening: Markham Gateway Dentistry provides professional in-office whitening treatments that can dramatically brighten your smile in just one visit. This procedure uses powerful whitening agents that are safe and effective.
  2. Take-Home Kits: For patients who prefer to whiten their teeth at home, the clinic offers custom take-home whitening kits. These kits include professional-grade whitening gel and custom trays for optimal results.

2. Dental Veneers

Dental veneers are thin shells made of porcelain or composite resin that are bonded to the front surface of teeth. They are an excellent solution for correcting various dental imperfections.

  1. Benefits of Veneers: Veneers can address issues such as gaps, chips, discoloration, and misalignment. They provide a natural-looking enhancement and can significantly improve the overall appearance of your smile.
  2. Quick and Effective: The process of getting veneers typically involves two visits to the clinic. During the first visit, your dentist will prepare your teeth and take impressions. The custom veneers are then crafted and placed during the second visit.

3. Orthodontics

While traditionally associated with teeth straightening, orthodontics also plays a crucial role in cosmetic dentistry.

  1. Invisalign: Markham Gateway Dentistry offers Invisalign, a clear aligner system that discreetly straightens teeth. This option is popular among adults and teens who want to improve their smile without the visibility of traditional braces.
  2. Traditional Braces: For those with more complex alignment issues, traditional metal braces may be recommended. The clinic provides comprehensive orthodontic evaluations to determine the best treatment option.
